The Never Ending Surf Trip

A surf trip to New Zealand that just never seemed to come to an end.

In late 2014, my girlfriend Jess and I embarked on a surf trip together to New Zealand for what was intended to be about a 6 month trip. We bought a one way ticket (somehow we didn't see this as a sign) with the intentions of buying our return ticket roughly 6 months later to allow us flexibility in our trip. Though we did indeed get a return ticket to fly home 6 months later, it wasn't long before we packed up our bags again and came right back to New Zealand.
